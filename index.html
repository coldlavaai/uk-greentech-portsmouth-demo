Our Story

Built on Trust,
Powered by Expertise

Founded in 2011, UK Greentech Portsmouth started as a one-man electrical operation run by company director Grant. What began with domestic rewires and small repairs in Portsmouth quickly grew through word-of-mouth recommendations and a reputation for doing things properly, first time, every time.

Today, UK Greentech Portsmouth is a multi-disciplined team of qualified electricians, renewable energy specialists, heating engineers and construction professionals. Based at our offices on Fisher Lane in Portsmouth, we've now completed over 6,000 projects across Portsmouth, Hampshire and Surrey — from single socket installations to full new-build developments.

What hasn't changed is our commitment to quality. Every project is delivered by our in-house team — no subcontractors, no shortcuts. That's how we maintain the standards and accountability our customers have come to rely on. We're proud to hold NAPIT, MCS, TrustMark and Checkatrade accreditations, and every member of our team is fully qualified, insured and DBS checked.
